Characters of God of War (2018)

The legendary reboot of the God of War saga

Kratos

The Ghost of Sparta and the game's main protagonist. Now older and living in the Norse realm of Midgard, Kratos is a more reserved father figure, teaching his son to survive and grow strong.

Atreus

Kratos' young son. Intelligent, curious, and brave, Atreus joins his father on their journey to fulfill his mother’s dying wish — to spread her ashes from the highest peak in all the realms.

Baldur

The main antagonist. A Norse god cursed with invulnerability and numbness to all physical and emotional sensations. He seeks Kratos and Atreus for personal vengeance and divine reasons.

Freya

A Vanir goddess, former queen, and powerful sorceress. She aids Kratos and Atreus throughout their journey, although her true identity and past reveal major plot twists.

The Huldra Brothers (Brok and Sindri)

Dwarven blacksmiths who provide upgrades and comic relief. Though constantly bickering, their skills and knowledge prove vital in Kratos and Atreus’s adventure.

Jörmungandr

The World Serpent. A massive creature from Norse mythology who assists Kratos and Atreus on their journey, communicating in ancient tongue and harboring mysterious origins.
