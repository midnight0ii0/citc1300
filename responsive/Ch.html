Kratos

The main protagonist, a Spartan warrior and the God of War. Once a servant of the Greek gods, Kratos now seeks redemption and a new life in the Norse realm, where he faces new challenges and enemies.

Atreus

Kratos' son, a curious and brave young boy. Atreus is eager to prove himself to his father and learn about his mysterious heritage, while also grappling with his own identity and destiny.

Freya

A Vanir goddess and ally to Kratos and Atreus. Freya is a powerful sorceress and former queen of the Valkyries, who aids the duo on their journey while harboring her own tragic past and secrets.
