Kratos

Kratos, the Ghost of Sparta, is the main protagonist of the God of War series. In the 2018 game, he embarks on a journey with his son, Atreus, to fulfill his late wife's final wish.

Atreus

Atreus is the son of Kratos and Faye. He is a curious and intelligent boy who learns about his heritage and the world around him during their journey.

Baldur

Baldur is a Norse god and the main antagonist of God of War 2018. He is invulnerable to all forms of harm, which becomes a central theme in the story.

Freya

Freya is a Vanir goddess and a key ally to Kratos and Atreus. She is a powerful sorceress with a tragic past that ties her to Baldur.

Mimir

Mimir, known as the smartest man alive, is a key companion in the game. He provides wisdom and guidance to Kratos and Atreus throughout their journey.
